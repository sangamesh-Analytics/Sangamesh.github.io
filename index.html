About Me

I'm Sangamesh—an aspiring Data Analyst who believes data should do more than fill dashboards; it should support clear, confident decisions.

I work with SQL, Python, and Power BI to analyze performance, uncover patterns, and translate complex datasets into insights that matter. My projects often focus on revenue analytics, customer behavior, and business metrics—areas where good analysis directly influences strategy.

My approach balances technical rigor with business context. I start with the problem, explore the data carefully, and deliver insights that are accurate, actionable, and easy to understand. I believe the best analysis isn’t the most complex—it’s the one that helps someone make a better decision.
